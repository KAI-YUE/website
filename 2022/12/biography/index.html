Biography

December 24, 2022 in Biography

I am Kai Yue, a Ph.D. student majored in Electrical Engineering at North Carolina State University. Currently, I am supervised by Prof. Chau-Wai Wong and Prof. Huaiyu Dai, focusing on the cross-disciplinary research of machine learning and communication. Before I joined NC State, I graduated with a B.E. in Electronic and Information Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China.

My research interests include but not limited to :

  • Federated Learning
  • Machine Learning
  • Statistical Learning
  • Image Processing
  • Video Coding
